AI Engineer

CureMD Pvt Ltd

Duration: Jul 2024 – Present

Summary: Building production AI features for healthcare—predictive modeling, clinical documentation agents, multimodal document understanding, and real-time voice bots—shipped across internal workflows and piloted for broader deployment.

  • Additional work across multimodal systems (LLMs + CV + Speech), API services (FastAPI), and data pipelines for safe deployment.
