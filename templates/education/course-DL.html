Deep Learning Specialization

DeepLearning.AI · Coursera

Certificate: View Credential

Overview: A 5-course, hands-on program by Andrew Ng and the DeepLearning.AI team covering the foundations of modern deep learning: neural networks, optimization & regularization, ML strategy, computer vision with CNNs, and sequence modeling (RNNs/LSTMs/GRUs, attention/transformers). Primary framework: TensorFlow/Keras.

Courses in the Specialization

  • 1) Neural Networks and Deep Learning — Forward/backpropagation, activation functions, vectorization, initialization, gradient descent; build and train basic deep nets for classification/regression.
  • 2) Improving Deep Neural Networks — Practical training techniques: hyperparameter tuning, L2 & dropout regularization, batch normalization, bias/variance diagnostics, and optimizers (Momentum, RMSProp, Adam). TensorFlow implementation workflows.
  • 3) Structuring Machine Learning Projects — Strategy and error analysis: setting up dev/test splits, diagnosing bias vs. variance, handling data mismatch, human-level baselines, and leveraging transfer/multi-task learning in real projects.
  • 4) Convolutional Neural Networks — Convolutions/pooling, modern architectures (e.g., residual networks), and applying CNNs to detection/recognition tasks across vision domains; includes practical applications like style transfer and face recognition concepts.
  • 5) Sequence Models — RNN/LSTM/GRU basics, word embeddings, seq2seq for translation, attention mechanisms, and exposure to transformer concepts; applications in speech, chatbots, and NLP.

Hands-on Highlights

  • Built dense networks from scratch and with Keras; practiced vectorized backprop and weight initialization.
